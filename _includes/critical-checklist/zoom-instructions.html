1. Navigate to a page on your website where the accordion component is used.
  2. Set the browser window width to 1080px.
  3. Enlarge the view of your screen up to 200%.
  4. Once zoomed, use a mouse to complete the tests in the checklist.

Use the following methods to control screen magnification in your browser:

  1. Windows OS: Click ctrl while using the scroll wheel on your mouse until you see "200%" appear in the top right of your screen. (To reset the zoom to the original value, click ctrl + 0.)
  2. Mac OS: Click command + + until you see "200%" appear in the top right of your screen. (To reset the zoom to the original value, click command + 0.)
  3. Browser settings: Customize your browser settings to display pages at 200%.
